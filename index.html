Most popular Ice Cream Flavors 2023

Cookies & Cream


Cookies & cream is a new take on vanilla, because it has chunks of cookies so your customers can eat a lot without getting bored with their ice cream. This is also great ice cream for waffle cones and ice cream sandwiches. Try making your own with this mix and waffle cone maker!
Toppings to try out: Peanut butter sauce. Anything to enhance the cookies already in the ice cream.

Mint Chocolate Chip

This minty ice cream is a favorite because it's unlike other ice creams. It's refreshing and light with sweet chocolate chunks. The flavors balance each other out so it's not too rich or too tart.
Toppings to try out: Chocolate sauce, Chocolate chips.

Strawberry

This fruit-based flavor is a classic ice cream that goes great on its own or in a banana split. It's sweet and creamy with chunks of strawberries. This ice cream is perfect for any treat! Try blending it into a milkshake and using these color-changing straws!
Toppings to try out: Rainbow sprinkles, chocolate chips
